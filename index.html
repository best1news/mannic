Mannik with berries and sour cream

Ingredients: 1 tbsp 1 tbsp ●Manka Kefir 1 st. Sugar 1 st. Flour 1 st. ●3 small eggs A pinch of salt Vanillin 1/3 hour Soda quenched 3/4 hrs ●soda quenched 3/4 hrs ●soda quenched ● any berries Cream: sour cream 150gr, sugar to taste. Cooking: 1. Mix jar with kefir, leave for 1 hour to swell. 2. Whisk the eggs with sugar, salt and vanilla, add them to the coin and whisk them. 3. Gradually add the flour and soda. Lubricate the mold, lay out the berries and pour in the dough. 4. Oven 40-60min at 170-180*. Check with a match. 5. Mix the sour cream with sugar while the mannik is baked (do not whip). 6. Cool the mannik, lubricate with cream.
